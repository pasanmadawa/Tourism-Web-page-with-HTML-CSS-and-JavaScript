Why Rambler?

Island like no other

South of the Indian subcontinent lies a small island called Sri Lanka. Some say it’s in a shape of a mango fruit; others refer to it as The Pearl of the Indian Ocean and the Teardrop of the Indian Ocean. No matter the name, Sri Lanka is a paradise on earth. It’s no wonder that Marco Polo called Sri Lanka “the finest island of its size in the entire world”. It is shaped like a jewel, and bears a variety of different treasures, from precious stones to stunning landscapes and wildlife. It’s surrounded by amazing sandy beaches protected by palm trees and guarded by the Indian Ocean.
