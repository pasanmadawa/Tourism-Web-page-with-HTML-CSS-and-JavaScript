HISTORICAL PLACES IN SRI LANKA

Temple of The Tooth

The temple of Tooth or Sri Dalada Maligawa is located in Kandy.The relic of the Lord Buddha is stored inside this historical place.this temple was built in 1500 centuary by ancient"s KIngs in sri lanka.This temple is surrounding by Udawatta Kale Santuary and Kandy Lake.The Paththirippuwa, the Octangle Pavillion is located at the enterace of the Maligawa.It gives a royal look to the Maligawa.In August month Kandy Esala Perahara is held on the Maliga Premises.We can see elepephats inside the Maliga premises, because lot of elephats are owned by Maligawa.

Lot of Historical things are stored in International Buddhist Museum.We can see historical and cultural things of countries like Thailand,Cambodia,China,Nepal etc in this museum.The Elephant Raja's stuffed remains are kept in a special museum which is located in Sri Dalada Maligawa.Specially local and foreign visitors come for looking for that Raja's body.UNESCO published Maligawa and Kandy city as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.This maligawa gives a honor and beautiful scenery to Kandy city.

Galle Fort

Galle Fort is located in Galle in Down South.It was built in 1588 by the Portuguese.It spred in 52 hectares.The Poetugues wanted to show their power to the King in that time.So that reason they built fortress around the sri lanka.The fort is made by using strong stones and strong cement mixure.More boundry line of fort are sorrounding by ocean.We can lot of portuguesse and dutch buildings and statues inside the fort.The fort has two entrence.The Galle Cricket ground is located near the fort.In the cricket match days lot of people gather to the fort, because we can see clear scenery in cricket ground into the fort.

The Clock Tower and The LIghthouse gives marvelous look to fort.The stones are used to build the clock tower.We can walk along the fort boundry line.The National Maritime Museum in Galle is located inside the Galle Fort.We can see mancient maps, naval craft, ropes, smoking pipes etc.In the Evening time we can see beautiful sunset in the Horizon.The Old Dutch Hospital, the Meera Jumma MOsque the ancient Buddhist temple and the All Saints Anglica Church are located inside the fort. Shallow beach area is located arraound the fort Lot of local and foreign visitors come everyday and enjoy .Galle fort give unique look to Galle city.UNESCO published Galle Fort as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Gai Viharaya is located in Polonnaruwa Secret city.We can see four images of the Buddha.They are seated image,standing image and reclining image.That image are made with stones.It is a marvelous creation by designer who lived in past.The standing image is 6.93m tall and reclining image is 14.12m long.The reclining image isone of the largest sculpture in Southeast Asia.

The environment arround the Gal Viharaya is very cal and quiet.Lot of Historical places are located arround the Gal Viharay.UNESCO published Galle Fort as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Dambulla Cave Temple

Dambulla Cave Temple is located in Dambulla which the city locate in central part of country.Five caves can see under the giant rock. The first cave which is located near the enterance , can see 14 meters long statue of the Buddha.The second cave, can see 56 statues of the Buddha.This cave is a lagest cave in this cave complex. This temple is situated to of the dambulla rock. In the top of the rock, can see vas area of the dambulla.

The Golden Buddh statue and Dambulla Golden Temple are located downhill from the Cave Temple.Lot of historical and colorful wall pictures are painted inside the caves.The environment inside the caves is very cool and calm.UNESCO published Dambulla cave temple as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Sigiriya

Sigiriya is located in central of Sri Lanka near to dambulla town.It was built by King Kashyapa.Sigiriya is a most marvelous and eye catched creation in Sri Lanka.This place used as a King's hide out place.This rock is 349m heist.A wonderful staircase was built through a lion's mouth to the top of the rock.Many water spouts were made in the terrace garden and top of the rock.The irrigation system in the sigiriya is a impressive one,because water system brings ground water to top of the rock.In rainy season , can see water spouts on the rock.We can see a 360 view on top of the rock.

We can see colorful frescoes middle part in the rock. This frescoes shows the cleverness of ancient people in the Sri Lanka. In the Mirror Wall, can see some poetries which written by people who came to visit this palace in ancient time. Around one million local and foreign visitors visit sigiriya annually.UNESCO published Sigiriya as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Adam's Peak

Adam's Peak or Sri Pada is located in central Sri Lanka near to Hatton town.It is a 2,243 m tall sacred mountain. Buddhist believe the printed footprints were printed by the Lord Buddha, Hindu people believe that footprints were printed by Hanuman or Shiva and Some islamic and Christian people believe that prints were printed by Adam or St.Thomas. Three enters ways are located into the Sri Pada. Famous way is Nallathanniya way.This path is located through the Nallathanniya village.We can see some waterfalls, tea plains,beautiful mountains way to the Nallathanniya.Maussakelle Reservoir is located near to the Sri Pada.In the top of the mountain,we can see 360 degree view in area. This scenery is very beautiful.

The peak pilgrimage season stars in December to May.Thousand of local and foreign visitors come to the Sri Pada daily in that season. We can see beautiful sunrise in morning time and can see conical shadow of Sri Pada in that time. This Sri Pada region is famous for butterflies, because lot of butterflies migrate to this region for their annual migration.Kalani, Kalu and Walawe river are starting from the Sri Pada. UNESCO published Adam's Peak as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
